Privacy Policy

1. Local-First Architecture

Lumina is designed as a "Local First" application. This means that all calculations, settings, and data processing occur entirely within your browser on your local machine. We do not have servers that store your trading data, position sizes, or strategy details.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information (PII) such as your name, address, or phone number. We do not require you to create an account to use the extension.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (such as the number of extension installs) through the browser intake stores (Chrome Web Store, Microsoft Edge Add-ons) to help us improve the product.

3. Broker Connections

Lumina never connects to your broker account.

We strictly operate as a calculator overlay. We cannot execute trades, view your account balance, or access your transaction history. Your funds and broker credentials remain completely isolated from our application.

4. Third-Party Services

Lumina overlays on top of TradingView.com. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to TradingView. Your use of TradingView is governed by their separate Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
